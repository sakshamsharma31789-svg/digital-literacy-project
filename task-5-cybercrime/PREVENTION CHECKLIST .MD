Check List for Safe Internet Use for College Students
1. Have strong and individual passwords.  Don't use your name, date of birth or 123456.  Use different passwords for each account. 
2. Enable two factor authentication (2FA) for your email, bank account and social media accounts. 
3. Do not click on links in emails, WhatsApp or SMS that seem suspicious (even if it looks official). 
4. Do not share personal information such as OTP, passwords, Aadhaar#, bank account information with anyone on the internet. 
5. Do not make use of the public Wi-Fi for banking/shopping or to log into accounts that may contain sensitive information. 
6. Only get apps from trusted sources such as the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. 

Financial Safety / Safety Tips for UPI 
1. Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests (pay requests).  Scammers frequently allow users to "receive" money via UPI. 
2. Never share YOU UPI PIN with anyone.  No Bank will ask for this.  
3. Verify the receiver's name before you send money via UPI. 
4. Be careful of fake customer support numbers on Google;  Always use official websites. 

What to Do If You Are a Victim of A Crime 
1. Immediately file a report on the National Cyber Crime Portal - https://cybercrime.gov.in 
2. Call to report on 1930 (a helpline dedicated to fraud related emergencies).

Responsible Social Media Use for College Students.

Do’s:

•	Think before you post. Ask yourself if it could affect your future.

•	Keep your accounts safe with passwords and private settings.

•	Share things that are helpful or positive and show who you are.

•	Respect what others think even if you don’t agree.

•	Check information before sharing so you don’t spread information.

Don’ts:

•	Don’t share stuff like your address, phone number or passwords.

•	Don’t post when you’re upset or emotional. You might regret it later.

•	Don’t argue online or bully others.

•	Don’t believe everything you see. Forward it without checking.

•	Don’t share much about your daily life. It can affect your privacy and safety.
